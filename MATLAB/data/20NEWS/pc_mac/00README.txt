PC vs. MAC from 20 newsgroups.

This is a binary dataset of comp.sys.ibm.pc.hardware (982) vs. comp.sys.mac.hardware (961) in the 20 newsgroups text dataset (18828 version).

graph_10NNw.mat:
A 10NN weighted graph (as in ICML paper eq 16).

Y.mat:
Class labels, a vector of length 1943.  PC=1, MAC=0.

name.txt:
The name of the documents.  The order of documents in graph_10NNw.mat and Y.mat is the same as in this file.
